features

chat with matrix

Syphon™️ works by using an encrypted and decentralized capable protocol called Matrix


end to end encrypted

Syphon™️ uses the olm/megolm encryption library provided by Matrix to keep your chats private from others

customize it

pick colors, themes, and eventually different widgets throughout the app according to your preference

no analytics

no analytics or tracking are present outside what matrix attempts to track on clients, but get help by emailing support@syphon.org

future goals

peer to peer messaging

host the server to one or all of your devices without the need for a homeserver

transfer data

transfer your data from one homeserver to another, or locally with P2P
